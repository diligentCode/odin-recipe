Samosa Recipe

Discription

Samosa is a popular deep-fried Indian snack made with a crispy outer crust and a spicy potato filling, often served with chutneys.

Ingridients

For Dought:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Carom seeds
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Water

For Filling

  • Boiled potatoes
  • Green peas
  • Cumin seeds
  • Ginger
  • Green chilies
  • Garam masala
  • Turmeric
  • Red chili powder
  • Salt

Steps To Prepare

  1. Prepare Dough: Mix flour, salt, carom seeds, and oil. Add water gradually and knead into a stiff dough. Rest for 30 minutes.

  2. Make Filling: Mash potatoes and saut√© with spices and peas. Let it cool.

  3. Shape & Fry: Roll the dough, cut into halves, form cones, fill with the mixture, seal, and deep-fry until golden brown.
