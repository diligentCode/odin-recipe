Matar Paneer

Discription

Matar Paneer is a creamy and mildly spiced North Indian curry made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and green peas in a tomato-based gravy.

Ingridients

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • Green peas
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Cumin seeds
  • Garam masala
  • Turmeric
  • Red chili powder
  • Cream
  • Oil
  • Salt
