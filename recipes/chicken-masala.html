Chicken Tikka Masala

Discription

Chicken Tikka Masala is a flavorful curry made with marinated, grilled chicken pieces cooked in a rich and spiced tomato-based sauce.

Ingridients

For Chicken Tikka:

  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Turmeric
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala
  • Lemon juice

For Sauce:

  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Cream
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • Garam masala
  • Turmeric
  • Coriander powder

Steps To Prepare

  1. Marinate Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt and spices. Marinate for 2 hours and grill or bake until cooked.

  2. Prepare Sauce: Saut√© onions and garlic. Add spices, tomato puree, and cook until thick. Add cream and simmer.

  3. Combine: Add grilled chicken to the sauce and simmer for 10 minutes. Garnish with cilantro.
